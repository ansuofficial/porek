History Of Porek Financial Institution

Porek financial is a financial institution founded in 2021, owned and operated by young dynamic entrepreneurs. It is legally registered institution in the Gambia to offer money transfer services and foreign exchange currencies . We are dedicated to provide a quality service to our customer and become more closer to them. Porek financial services and bureau dechange headquarter is located at Kerr serign, west coast Region, The Gambia. We are currently operating more than ten (10) branches across the country approve by the Central bank of the GAMBIA with the vision to be key player in the market in few years time.
